# THE MOON RECORDS — MANIFESTO

---

```
THE FIELD IS OPEN.
THE LIGHT COMES FROM ELSEWHERE.
THAT IS THE ENTIRE POINT.
```

---

The Moon Records is a record label.

It exists to release music that holds still long enough to be heard.

Not ambient as background. Not ambient as function. Not ambient as the filler between other things. The music released here is the thing itself — a transmission that asks for nothing except attention, and rewards that attention slowly, on its own terms.

The label takes its name from Logic Moon — the artist who defined its initial sound and position. From that single starting point, the scope opened: ambient, drone, field recordings, lo-fi texture, deep soundscape. What unites these forms is not a genre boundary but a shared refusal. The refusal to rush. The refusal to assert. The commitment to letting sound accumulate at the rate it requires.

---

```
THE QUESTION IS SIMPLE:
— DOES THE MUSIC KNOW WHAT IT IS?
```

---

The moon does not generate light. It reflects it — cold, distant, transformed by its passage through space and atmosphere. The best ambient music operates similarly. It does not impose. It receives, holds, and returns something changed. A room at 3am. A field in winter. A frequency that was always present but required silence to become audible.

This is the register of the label. Everything released here exists in that space.

---

The infrastructure is code.

Decisions are versioned. Documents are committed. The git history is the label's memory — every direction, every release, every evolution of thinking preserved in the log. This is not a technical affectation. It is the honest architecture of a label that takes its own decisions seriously enough to write them down.

The OS is open. Anyone can read how this label operates, what it signs, what it refuses, how it thinks. Anyone can fork the structure.

A fork carries the method. It does not carry the history. The history belongs to the original.

---

```
WHAT THIS IS NOT:
— NOT BACKGROUND MUSIC
— NOT FUNCTIONAL ATMOSPHERE
— NOT CONTENT
— NOT THERAPY
— NOT NEW AGE RESOLUTION
```

---

The moon does not resolve. It cycles. Full → waning → dark → waxing → full. No phase is the correct phase. No phase is the destination. The cycle is the point.

A release on The Moon Records does not need to arrive anywhere. It needs to move — slowly, with intention, through a space the listener was not aware of occupying until the music made it visible.
